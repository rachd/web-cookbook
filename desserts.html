Almond Biscotti

makes 20 biscotti

from food.com

Ingredients

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 1 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1/8 teaspoon salt
  • 3 eggs
  • 2 tablespoons amaretto or 2 tablespoons rum with 1 teaspoon almond extract
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla
  • 1 cup almonds, toasted and roughly chopped

Directions

  1. Preheat oven to 300 degrees
  2. Line 2 baking sheets with parchment
  3. Combine flour, sugar, baking powder, and salt
  4. In separate bowl, whisk together eggs, amaretto, and vanilla extract
  5. Add dry ingredients and mix until well combined (dough should be sticky)
  6. Scrape dough out onto pan
  7. Shape until long, flat loaf 10 inches long and 5 inches wide
  8. Bake until firm and dry, about 50 minutes
  9. Remove from oven and let cool 10 minutes
  10. Using long, serrated knife, slice into 1/2-inch wide slices
  11. Bake slices 20 minutes, turn over, then bake 20 more minutes

Lemon Bars

Ingredients

  • 1 1/2 cups + 3 tbsp flour
  • 1/2 cup + 1 tbsp confectioners' sugar
  • 3/4 cup butter (1 1/2 sticks)
  • 2 lemons
  • 3 eggs
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 1/2 tsp baking powder
  • 1/2 tsp salt

Directions

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees
  2. Line 13x9 inche baking pan with parchment paper
  3. Lightly grease parchment paper
  4. In medium bowl, combine flour and confectioners' sugar
  5. Cut in butter
  6. Sprinkle dough in pan and pat down
  7. Bake 15-17 minutes
  8. Grate 1 tsp lemon rind and squeeze 1/3 cup juice
  9. In large bowl with mixer on high, beat eggs until thick and lemon colored, about 3 minutes
  10. Reduce speed to low and add lemon peel, lemon juice, sugar, baking powder, salt, and 3 tablespoons flour
  11. Beat until blended
  12. Pour over warm crust
  13. Bake about 15 minutes
  14. Sift 1 tbsp confectioners' sugar over top
  15. Cool completely in pan

Sugar Cookies

Ingredients

  • 1 1/3 cups flour
  • 3/4 tsp baking powder
  • 1/4 tsp salt
  • 1/2 cup butter (1 stick)
  • 1 egg
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract

Directions

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees
  2. In small bowl, combine flour, baking powder, and salt
  3. In large bowl with mixer on medium speed, beat butter and sugar until light and fluffy
  4. Beat in egg and vanilla until blended
  5. Reduce speed to low and beat in flour mixture until just combined
  6. Drop dough by heaping teaspoons, 2 inches apart, on ungreased cookie sheets
  7. Flatten dough
  8. Bake 10-12 minutes
  9. Cook on wire rack
