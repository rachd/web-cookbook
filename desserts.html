Apple Pie

Makes one pie

Ingredients

  • 1 recipe pie crust
  • 2 1/2 lbs tart apples, about 8 large
  • 1/2 cup granulated sugar
  • 1 1/2 teaspoon cinnammon
  • pinch of salt
  • 5 tablespoons all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 cup light brown sugar
  • 1 tablespoons butter
  • 3 drops almond extract

Directions

  1. Peel, core, and slice the apples
  2. Mix the sugar, cinnamon, and salt in a large bowl and toss in the apples, mixing well
  3. Chill for at least an hour or overnight
  4. Drain the liquid from the apple and cook until liquid is reduced to 1/2 its volume
  5. Mix the flour and brown sugar and add to apple liquid with butter and extract
  6. Cook in saucepan over low head, stirring slowly for about 5 minutes
  7. Pour this over apples and toss well
  8. Roll pie crust into two pieces
  9. Line a 9" pie pan with 1 piece and place filling on dough
  10. Cover with remaining dough and crimp edges
  11. Bake at 375 degrees for 35-40 minutes

Almond Biscotti

makes 20 biscotti

from food.com

Ingredients

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 1 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1/8 teaspoon salt
  • 3 eggs
  • 2 tablespoons amaretto or 2 tablespoons rum with 1 teaspoon almond extract
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla
  • 1 cup almonds, toasted and roughly chopped

Directions

  1. Preheat oven to 300 degrees
  2. Line 2 baking sheets with parchment
  3. Combine flour, sugar, baking powder, and salt
  4. In separate bowl, whisk together eggs, amaretto, and vanilla extract
  5. Add dry ingredients and mix until well combined (dough should be sticky)
  6. Scrape dough out onto pan
  7. Shape until long, flat loaf 10 inches long and 5 inches wide
  8. Bake until firm and dry, about 50 minutes
  9. Remove from oven and let cool 10 minutes
  10. Using long, serrated knife, slice into 1/2-inch wide slices
  11. Bake slices 20 minutes, turn over, then bake 20 more minutes

Lemon Bars

Ingredients

  • 1 1/2 cups + 3 tbsp flour
  • 1/2 cup + 1 tbsp confectioners' sugar
  • 3/4 cup butter (1 1/2 sticks)
  • 2 lemons
  • 3 eggs
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 1/2 tsp baking powder
  • 1/2 tsp salt

Directions

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees
  2. Line 13x9 inch baking pan with parchment paper
  3. Lightly grease parchment paper
  4. In medium bowl, combine flour and confectioners' sugar
  5. Cut in butter
  6. Sprinkle dough in pan and pat down
  7. Bake 15-17 minutes
  8. Grate 1 tsp lemon rind and squeeze 1/3 cup juice
  9. In large bowl with mixer on high, beat eggs until thick and lemon colored, about 3 minutes
  10. Reduce speed to low and add lemon peel, lemon juice, sugar, baking powder, salt, and 3 tablespoons flour
  11. Beat until blended
  12. Pour over warm crust
  13. Bake about 15 minutes
  14. Sift 1 tbsp confectioners' sugar over top
  15. Cool completely in pan

Mexican Hot Chocolate Cookies

From LaurenConrad.com

Ingredients

  • 1 cup sugar
  • 1/2 cup canola oil
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 tablespoon vanilla extract
  • 3/4 cup flour
  • 3/4 cup cocoa powder
  • 2 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground cayenne pepper
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1 cup chocolate chips

Directions

  1. In a large bowl, whisk together sugar and canola oil
  2. Whisk in eggs and vanilla until thoroughly combined
  3. In a separate bowl, combine all the dry ingredients
  4. Add the dry ingredients to the wet and mix until just combined
  5. Scoop tablespoon-sized cookies onto greased cookie sheet
  6. Set in fridge for 10 to 15 minutes
  7. Preheat oven to 350 degrees
  8. Bake cookies for 8 minutes

Pie Crust

Makes crust for a 9" 2-crust pie

Ingredients

  • 1 3/4 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 2/3 cup shortening
  • 1/3 cup ice water

Directions

  1. Sift flour and salt into mixing bowl
  2. Cut in shortening with pastry cutter until the bits are the size of peas
  3. Sprinkle ice water in by tablespoonfuls, stirring in with a fork, until the dough can be shaped into a ball
  4. Wrap the dough in wax paper and chill for at least 1 hour

Pumpkin Cinnamon Rolls

Makes 10-12 rolls

Ingredients

    For the dough
  • 3 1/2 cups bread flour
  • 1/4 cup granulated sugar
  • 1/4 cup firmly packed dark brown sugar
  • 1 tablespoon instant dry yeast
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon cinnamon
  • 1/4 teaspoon ground ginger
  • 1/4 teaspoon ground cardamom
  • 3 ounces unsalted butter
  • 2/3 cup whole milk
  • 1 large egg
  • 2/3 cup pumpkin puree
    • For the filling
  • 3/4 cup firmly packed light brown sugar
  • 1/4 cup granulated sugar
  • 1/2 teaspoon cinnamon
  • 1/4 teaspoon ground cloves
  • 1/4 teaspoon freshly grated nutmeg
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1 ounce unsalted butter, melted
    • For the assembly
  • 1 ounce unsalted butter, melted
    • For the cream cheese frosting
  • 2 ounces cream cheese, softened
  • 3 tablespoons well-shaken buttermilk
  • 1 1/4 cups confectioner's sugar, sifted

Directions

  1. Butter one 10-inch round cake pan
  2. Line the bottom with parchment paper and butter the parchment
  3. Dust with flour, knocking out any extra
  4. Mix flour sugars, yeast, salt, cinnamon, ginger, and cardamom on medium
  5. Add butter and mix until incorporated, about 1 minute
  6. Add the milk and egg and mix on low until incorporated
  7. Add pumpkin puree and mix on medium for 3 minutes
  8. Form dough into large ball and place in clean, lightly greased bowl
  9. Cover with plastic wrap and let rest for 30 minutes
  10. In a small bowl, stir together sugars, cinnamon, cloves, nutmeg, and salt
  11. Add melted butter and stir until combined
  12. Dust a work surface with a sprinkling of flour
  13. Using a rolling pin, roll dough into a large rectangle about 20x10 inches
  14. Brush dough with half the melted butter and sprinkle with filling, leaving a 1/4 inch border
  15. Roll up long side of the rectangle
  16. Slice the log and place rolls into baking pan
  17. Brush the tops of the rolls with rest of melted butter
  18. Cover with plastic wrap and set aside until nearly doubled, about 45 minutes
  19. Preheat oven to 350 degrees
  20. Bake for 25-30 minutes
  21. In mixing bowl, beat cream cheese and buttermilk on medium until lump free
  22. Add confectioner's sugar and beat on medium-low until smooth
  23. Pour frosting over warm rolls and serve immediately

Sugar Cookies

Ingredients

  • 1 1/3 cups flour
  • 3/4 tsp baking powder
  • 1/4 tsp salt
  • 1/2 cup butter (1 stick)
  • 1 egg
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract

Directions

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees
  2. In small bowl, combine flour, baking powder, and salt
  3. In large bowl with mixer on medium speed, beat butter and sugar until light and fluffy
  4. Beat in egg and vanilla until blended
  5. Reduce speed to low and beat in flour mixture until just combined
  6. Drop dough by heaping teaspoons, 2 inches apart, on ungreased cookie sheets
  7. Flatten dough
  8. Bake 10-12 minutes
  9. Cook on wire rack
