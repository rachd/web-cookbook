Asian Grilled Chicken

Ingredients

  • 1 chicken, separated
  • 1/8 cup honey
  • 1/8 cup maple syrup
  • 1/4 cup vegetable oil
  • 1 tablespoon sesame oil
  • 1/4 cup lemon juice
  • 1/2 teaspoon minced garlic
  • 1/2 teaspoon red pepper flakes
  • 1/4 teaspoon ginger

Directions

  1. Whisk together all ingredients except the chicken.
  2. Pour marinade over chicken and let sit for at least 30 minutes.
  3. Grill the chicken over medium heat for about 30 minutes or until cooked.

Chicken Pot Pie

Makes 2 pies

Ingredients

  • pie crusts for two 9-inch pies
  • 1 cup chopped celery
  • 1 cup chopped carrot
  • 1 cup chopped potato
  • 1/3 cup butter
  • 1/2 cup all-purpose flour, sifted
  • 2 cups chicken broth
  • 1 cup half and half
  • 2 cups chicken, cooked, chopped
  • 1 cup peas
  • salt and pepper to taste

Directions

  1. Preheat oven to 375.
  2. In large frying pan, saute celery, carrots, and potato in the butter.
  3. Add flour and stir until smooth. Cook one minute, stirring constantly.
  4. Add chicken broth and half and half, stirring until thickened and bubbly.
  5. Stir in chicken, peas, salt, and pepper. Allow to cool.
  6. Roll out pie crusts and place bottom crust in pan.
  7. Pour in filling and place top crust. Fold over edges and crimp.
  8. Poke slits in top and brush with milk
  9. Bake uncovered for thirty minutes then cover with foil and bake 30 minutes
  10. Cool 10 minutes before eating
