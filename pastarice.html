Lasagna

makes 12-15 servings

Ingredients

  • 1 1-lb package lasagna, uncooked (16 noodles)
  • 1 16-oz container ricotta cheese
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 lb bulk Italian sausage, cooked and drained, out of casing
  • 2 jars tomato and basil pasta sauce
  • 4 cups (1 lb) shredded mozzarella cheese

Directions

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees
  2. Cook lasagna noodles as package directs
  3. In bowl, mix ricotta, Parmesan, and eggs well
  4. Grease bottom of 13x9 inch pan
  5. Spread 1/2 cup pasta sauce
  6. Top with 4 noodles, 1/3 ricotta mix, 1/3 sausage, 1/3 pasta sauce, 1/3 mozzarella
  7. Repeat layering twice, ending with noodles, sauce, mozzarella
  8. Cover and bake 1 hour
  9. Uncover and let stand 15 minutes before serving
