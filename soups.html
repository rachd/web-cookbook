Butternut Squash Soup

Serves 6

from wholefoodsmarket.com

Ingredients

  • 2 tablespoons extra-virgin olive oil
  • 1 carrot, diced
  • 1 celery stalk, diced
  • 1 onion, diced
  • 4 cups cubed butternut squash
  • 1/2 teaspoon chopped fresh thyme
  • 4 cups low-sodium chicken broth
  • 1/2 teaspoon fine sea salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground black pepper

Directions

  1. Heat oil in large soup pot
  2. Add carrot, celery, and onion
  3. Cook until vegetables have begun to soften and onion turns translucent, 3-4 minutes
  4. Stir in butternut squash, thyme, chicken broth, salt, and pepper
  5. Bring to a boil, reduce heat, and simmer until squash is fork-tender, about 30 minutes
  6. Use an immersion blender to puree soup or let cool slightly and use an upright blender
