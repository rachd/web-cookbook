Mashed Potatoes

Makes 10-12 servings

Ingredients

  • 3.5 pounds russet potatoes
  • 2 tablespoons kosher salt
  • 2 cups half and half (16 oz)
  • 6 cloves garlic, minced

Directions

  1. Peel and dice potatoes.
  2. Put potatoes and salt in a large saucepan and cover with water.
  3. Bring to a boil over medium-high heat.
  4. Reduce heat, maintaining a rolling boil, until potatoes are soft.
  5. Heat half and half and garlic in medium saucepan over medium heat until simmering.
  6. Remove from heat and set aside.
  7. Remove potatoes from heat and drain water.
  8. Mash and stir in garlic-cream mixture
  9. Let stand for 5 minutes then serve

Sichuan-Style Eggplant

from seriouseats.com

Ingredients

  • kosher salt
  • 1 1/2 pounds chinese eggplant, quartered and cut into 3-4-inch lengths
  • 2 red Thai bird chilis
  • 3 tablespoons rice wine vinegar
  • 2 tablespoons Shaoxing wine
  • 1 tablespoon sugar
  • 2 teaspoons soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon Chiankiang vinegar
  • 1 1/4 teaspoons cornstarch
  • 3 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • 4 teaspoons minced fresh ginger
  • 4 cloves minced garlic
  • 4 scallions, whites thinly sliced, greens cut into 1/3-inch segments
  • 2 tablespoons Sichuan chili broad bean paste (Doubanjiang)
  • roughly chopped fresh cilantro for garnish

Directions

  1. Combine 1/2 cup kosher salt with 2 quarts water in a medium bowl
  2. Soak eggplant pieces skin-side-up for 10-20 minutes
  3. Meanwhile, heat rice wine vinegar in small saucepan until simmering
  4. Place sliced chilis in small bowl and pour hot vinegar on top
  5. Let rest for 5 minutes, then add wine, sugar, soy sauce, and Chinkiang vinegar
  6. Add cornstarch and stir until dissolved. Set aside.
  7. Drain eggplant and pat dry with paper towels
  8. Heat oil in wok over high heat
  9. Reduce heat to medium and add eggplant, cooking until softened and browned
  10. Push eggplant to side of wok and return wok to high heat
  11. Add ginger, garlic, and scallions
  12. Stir constantly for about 30 seconds
  13. Add bean paste and stir for about 30 seconds
  14. Pour in chili sauce, scraping in any sugars left in bottom
  15. Stir constantly until sauce is thickened and glossy, 1-3 minutes

Sesame-Cucumber Salad

serves 4 to 6

from oprah.com

Ingredients

  • 4 english cucumbers
  • 3/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1 tablespoon sugar
  • 1/2 cup rice vinegar
  • 3 cloves minced garlic
  • 1 teaspoon red chili flakes
  • 1 tablespoon sesame seeds
  • 6 green onions

Directions

  1. Thinly slice cucumbers crosswise
  2. Toss cucumbers, salt, and sugar in large bowl
  3. Let cucumbers sit for 30 minutes
  4. Transfer to colander, rinse, and drain well, pressing out extra moisture
  5. In serving bowl, stir together vinegar, garlic, chili flakes, and sesame seeds
  6. Add cucumbers and toss to coat
  7. Thinly slice green onions and sprinkle over top
  8. Cover and refrigerate at least 2 hours

Vegetable-Stuffed Eggplant Rollatini

from yummyaddiction.com

Ingredients

  • 2 eggplants
  • 1/2 cup diced onion
  • 4 cloves minced garlic
  • 1 1/3 cup diced celery (about 3 stalks)
  • 1 14.5 ounce can diced tomatoes, undrained
  • 1/3 cup parley, chopped
  • olive oil
  • salt and pepper

Directions

  1. Slice the eggplants into 1/4-inch slices
  2. Salt slices and let sit for 15 minutes
  3. In medium pan over medium heat, heat five tablespoons of oil
  4. Add onion, garlic, and celery and cook for 3 minutes, until onion is translucent
  5. Add tomatoes and parsley and cook for 5 minutes
  6. Season to taste with salt and pepper
  7. Rinse salt off eggplant and pay dry with paper towels
  8. Preheat grill to medium or heat grill pan over medium-high
  9. Brush both sides of eggplant with olive oil and sprinkle with salt and pepper
  10. Grill on both sides, about 2-3 minutes a side
  11. Preheat over to 375 degrees
  12. Grease 11x7 baking pan
  13. Fill eggplants, arranging in pan
  14. Top with remaining filling and bake uncovered for 15 minutes
